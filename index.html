Calendur

Clean, colour-coded calendars for schools & events. Export SVG, PNG or JPG at any resolution.

Calendar

Weekend days

Colour codes

Assign a colour + label to each type (holiday, break, exam…). These appear in the legend.

Events & breaks

Add single days or ranges. A range fills every day between the two dates.

Paste CSV or tab-separated data. First row is a header (Name, Category, Start, End).

Paste a full Calendur .json config, or an array of events: [{"name":"…","category":"…","start":"…","end":"…"}]

Click to add common dates for this calendar year.

Paste a GitHub raw URL (e.g. raw.githubusercontent.com/…/events.json) or a repo path.

Supports .json (Calendur config or array of events) and .csv files.

Notes & descriptions

Add freeform descriptions, policies, or context about breaks and holidays. Appears at the bottom of the exported calendar.

Export

Design inspired by nagusamecs.github.io — aesthetic used with appreciation. Built with vanilla JS; no dependencies, no telemetry, fully client-side. Calendars are generated in your browser — nothing is ever uploaded.
